Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer local home delivery within Balangir region. Please call us to confirm delivery availability.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay), and other local digital methods.

3. Can I return or exchange medicines?

We accept returns only for damaged or incorrect items within 48 hours of delivery. Please retain your bill.

4. Do you sell prescription medicines?

We do not sell prescription-only medicines online. You may visit our store with a valid prescription.

5. How do I contact support?

You can call us directly or use the Contact Us page.
